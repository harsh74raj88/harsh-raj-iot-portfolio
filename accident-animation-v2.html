System Working Flow

1. Normal Monitoring

ESP32 reads IMU and GPS values continuously while vehicle is moving.

2. Impact / Tilt Detected

Acceleration spike, gyroscope rotation and tilt indicate accident-like motion.

3. Accident Confirmed

System confirms accident after logic check and activates buzzer/OLED warning.

4. GPS Location Captured

Latitude, longitude, speed and Google Maps link are prepared.

5. Alerts Sent

Telegram, SMS and LoRa messages are sent to contacts/gateway.

6. Emergency Response

Receiver gets location and can take action quickly.
